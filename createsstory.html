Chapter 1
There was once a boy, far above east, herding a den of sheep, protecting it from any danger. The boy lived right next to tolsken, a small town with a tightly closed community. One day as some sort of a joke he decides to go into town calling danger had come upon his sheep, and in an instance the pople of tolsken respond with quickness. But Once they go to the sheep and saw all was well.
The people disgruntled warned him about falsely saying things, but as time passed the boy decided to partake in the joke again, and again the people came to his aid. He repeated the joke, again and again and again. Until, One summer day, from the bushes, watching it's prey, waiting for the right moment, a wolf, looking for its next meal. Out of the glimmer of the sun, the boy spotted the wolf, he ran into town crying, but the people of tolsken became deaf to his cries. He ran back into the pastures, deciding to doing any measure in protecting his herd, but the wolf had already left with his prey. \
